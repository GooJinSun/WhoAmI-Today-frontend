Child Safety Policy

We are committed to ensuring the safety and well-being of children who interact with our app. This Child Safety Policy outlines our practices and responsibilities to prevent the distribution of Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) and comply with all relevant laws and regulations.

Our Commitment

  • Actively monitoring and removing any inappropriate content that violates child safety guidelines.
  • Reporting any instances of CSAM or child exploitation to the relevant authorities immediately.
  • Providing users with tools to report any suspected violations directly through the app or website.
  • Ensuring that our team members are trained to handle sensitive issues related to child safety.

Reporting and Contact

If you encounter any content or behavior that raises concerns about child safety, please report it immediately through our contact email or use the in-app reporting tool.

Reports are treated with the utmost confidentiality and will be reviewed by our team promptly.

Compliance with Laws

We comply with all local and international laws and regulations regarding child safety, including the prevention of child exploitation and abuse. We work closely with law enforcement and child protection organizations to ensure compliance and support investigations.
